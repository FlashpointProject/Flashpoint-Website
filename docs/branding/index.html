Branding
Boiled down to its simplest form, the Flashpoint logo is the project's initials flipped against each other. The blank space forms an arrow shape that can be interpreted as a play button or download symbol, a reminder of the processes we've grown accustomed to when accessing our favorite games. The wording typically seen alongside the logo uses the Tahoma font.
Flashpoint's logo falls under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 license, meaning it can be freely modified and redistributed so long as Seirade (the logo's designer) or BlueMaxima's Flashpoint receive credit.
A package containing the vectorized logo in various styles may be downloaded here.
